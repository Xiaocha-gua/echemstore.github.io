Anion Exchange Membrane

High Performance · Long Life · Cost Effective

Product Overview

echemstore Anion Exchange Membrane (AEM) is specifically designed for water electrolysis hydrogen production applications, utilizing advanced polymer materials and manufacturing processes with excellent OH⁻ ion conductivity and outstanding chemical stability. As a core component of next-generation water electrolysis technology, we aim to provide customers with high-performance, long-life anion exchange membrane products.

Key Features

  • High Ion Conductivity: OH⁻ ion conductivity reaches 170 mS/cm, ensuring excellent electrolysis performance
  • Excellent Stability: Outstanding chemical stability in alkaline environments, ensuring long-term use
  • Low Swelling: Swelling rate ≤15%, maintaining membrane mechanical strength and dimensional stability
  • Large Width Production: Maximum width up to 1m, meeting large-scale application requirements
  • Customizable: Available in different thicknesses and size specifications, adapting to various application scenarios
