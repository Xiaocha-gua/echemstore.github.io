Economical Water/CO2 Electrolysis Test Platform

Cost-effective · Dual System Coverage · Stable & Reliable

Product Overview

This Economical Water/CO2 Electrolysis Test Platform is a cost-effective electrochemical testing equipment specifically designed for research institutions, university laboratories, and small to medium-sized enterprises. Focusing on two core systems: water electrolysis (AEM/PEM/AWE) and CO2 electrolysis (CO₂RR), it meets diverse testing needs for basic research, material screening, and reaction mechanism exploration.

The product's core advantages are "high cost-effectiveness + stable reliability + convenient operation". While ensuring uncompromised core testing performance, it significantly reduces procurement costs, breaking the price barrier of high-end electrochemical testing equipment and providing strong support for low-cost scientific research.

Core Advantages

  • Outstanding Cost-effectiveness: Single/dual-channel water electrolysis offers high cost-performance, significantly reducing research investment.
  • Comprehensive Dual System Coverage: Perfectly compatible with both water electrolysis (HER, OER, overall water splitting) and CO2 electrolysis systems, switchable without additional configuration.
  • Stable and Reliable Performance: Integrated liquid replenishment device supports long-term continuous testing, ensuring repeatability and credibility of experimental data.
  • Strong Compatibility & Flexibility: Compatible with three-electrode systems, H-type electrolytic cells, flow electrolytic cells, and other devices. Integrated hardware and software design, ready to use out of the box.
